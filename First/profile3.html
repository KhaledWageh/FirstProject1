What is the meaning of Energy Resources?

An energy resource is something that can produce heat, power life, move objects, or produce electricity. Matter that stores energy is called a fuel. Human energy consumption has grown steadily throughout human history.

What is the Energy Resources?

There are 2 types of Energy Resources
  • Renewable Energy
  • Nonrenewable Energy

Renewable Energy Examples

  1. Solar Energy
  2. Wind Energy
  3. Hydroelectricity

Nonrenewable Energy Examples

  1. Oil
  2. Coal
  3. Natural Gas
  4. Nuclear Energy
